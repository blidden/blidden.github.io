FINSIA

A comprehensive redesign of FINSIA's webiste and member portal.

FINSIA – Financial Services Institute of Australasia is a not-for-profit membership association for financial services professionals in Australia and New Zealand. They’re an advocate for the financial services profession and provide practitioners with professional qualifications and continous professional development (CPD).

Deloitte Digital acted as consultants for their membership portal and public-facing website redesign. At the time their current digital offerings were aging. Their revised brand identity gave way for a new web presence to be forged.

Digital offerings prior to redesign

Their membership portal had a slightly more contemporary aestheitc however, in order to maintain brand and visual consistency, Deloitte Digital were commissioned to include it for the engagement.

Design Treatment

Combining modern patterns and the professional yet delicate brand identity of FINSIA together produced a clean and minimal aesthetic. Accents of yellow serve as a subtle motif throughout the designs, breaking the ubiquitous nature of blues in modern branding. A responsive design was also produced, ensuring users on smaller devices could maintain full functionality and usability.
